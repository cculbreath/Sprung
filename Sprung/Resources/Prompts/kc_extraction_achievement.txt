## Extract These Elements

### Achievement Details
- What was achieved
- When and where
- Context and significance
- Your specific contribution

### Evidence
- Documentation (publication, certificate, etc.)
- Recognition received
- Impact metrics
- Third-party validation

---

## Output Format

Return JSON matching StructuredKnowledgeCard schema with card_type="achievement".

Include:
- evidence_blocks with documentation references
- facts.outcomes showing impact
- 1-2 resume_bullets summarizing the achievement
