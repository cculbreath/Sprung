# Employment Card Deep Extraction

Extract comprehensive employment card for:
**{TITLE}** at **{ORGANIZATION}** ({DATE_RANGE})

## Source Documents
{EVIDENCE_BLOCKS_WITH_CONTENT}

---

## Extract These Elements

### Scope & Structure
- Your title and how it evolved
- Team size and your role within it (IC, lead, manager)
- Reporting structure
- Budget or resource responsibility
- Geographic scope

### Responsibilities (be specific)
- Day-to-day activities
- Key ownership areas
- Cross-functional interactions
- Decision-making authority

### Projects & Initiatives (name them)
For each significant project:
- Project name
- Your specific role/contribution
- Technologies used
- Outcome/deliverable
- Timeline

### Technologies & Methods
- Languages, frameworks, tools
- Methodologies
- Platforms and infrastructure

### Quantified Outcomes
- Revenue/cost impact
- Efficiency improvements
- Scale metrics
- Team metrics

### Growth & Learning
- Skills developed
- Promotions/expanded responsibility
- Challenges overcome

---
